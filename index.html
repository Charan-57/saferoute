About SafeRoute AI
SafeRoute AI helps users choose safer travel routes using AI risk scoring, community crime reports, and OpenStreetMap data. Designed for students, women, night-shift workers, and solo travelers.

How to Use
1. Enter Source & Destination
2. Click Find Safe Route
3. View green (safe) and red (unsafe) paths
4. Toggle Crime Heatmap
5. Click map to report incidents
6. Use SOS for emergencies

Built with AI + OpenStreetMap + Community Safety
